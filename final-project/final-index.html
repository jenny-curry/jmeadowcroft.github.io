Welcome to All About Rabbits!

Today you will learn all about the magical animal that is the domesticated pet that is known as a rabbit, or a bunny. These are beautiful kind hearted creatures that may be your idea pet.

Is a Rabbit the Pet for You?

Rabbits are loving creatures, but they do require care, such as quality time, brushing, and nail trimming. You can see more details about this on the Care Page.

What is the Care Page?

The care page contains a multitude of information, and everything you need to know about how to care for your new pet rabbit.
